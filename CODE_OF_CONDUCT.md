# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

This project expects respectful, direct, and constructive collaboration. Harassment, discrimination, intimidation, and bad-faith disruption are not acceptable.

## Expected Behavior

- Assume good intent, but be precise.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Prefer evidence, reproductions, and concrete reasoning.
- Keep discussions relevant to the project and its civilian research scope.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or insulting language
- Harassment or discrimination
- Deliberate disruption of discussions or reviews
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, reject contributions, or block participants who violate these expectations.

## Reporting

Open a private maintainer contact path before posting sensitive moderation details in public threads.
